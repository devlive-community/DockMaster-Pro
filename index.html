Everything You Need

Powerful features to supercharge your macOS workflow.

⚡

Multi-Row Dock

Break free from the single-row limitation. Display your apps in a customizable grid layout with multiple rows and columns.

📁

Smart Sections

Organize apps into named sections. Add, rename, reorder, and delete sections to keep your dock perfectly organized.

🎨

Theme Engine

Full theme customization with colors, corner radius, blur, and shadows. Import and export themes as .dmtheme files.

🖥️

Workspaces

Create multiple workspaces, each with its own dock layout. Bind to macOS Spaces for automatic switching.

✨

20+ Animations

Bounce, Shake, Pulse, Flip 3D, Glitch, and more. Customize hover, launch, and quit animations for each app.

🔍

Quick Search

Spotlight-like search panel for apps, files, documents, and recent items. Open with Option+Space.

📊

System Monitor

Built-in CPU, memory, and network speed widget. Tap to expand and see detailed system metrics.

🌍

4 Languages

English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and Japanese. Switch languages in-app without restarting.

🔒

Privacy First

Open source, no tracking, no analytics. Your data stays on your Mac. Only requires Accessibility permission.
